Archive Class¶

The Archive class is the primary mechanism for opening PSRFITS files

Methods¶

load(filename[, prepare=True, baseline_removal=True])¶

Load a PSRFITS file, process the metadata, and form the data arrays.

Parameters:
  • prepare (bool) – Rotate the pulse so that the peak is in the center of phase. Stores relevant time delays associated with the rotation.
  • baseline_removal (bool) – Subtract the baseline intensity of the average profile off-pulse region from all individual data profiles.
Returns:

None
